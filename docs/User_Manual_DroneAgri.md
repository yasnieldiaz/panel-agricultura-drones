## 1. Introduction

DroneAgri Panel is a web and mobile application that allows customers to request agricultural drone services quickly and easily. The platform is available in 6 languages: Spanish, English, Polish, Czech, Slovak, and Italian.

### Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Internet connection (the app works partially offline)
- Registered user account

---

## 2. Registration and Login

### Create a New Account

1. Go to the main page: https://cieniowanie.droneagri.pl
2. Click on **"Log In"** in the upper right corner
3. Select **"Register"**
4. Complete the form:
   - **Full name**
   - **Email address**
   - **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Log In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Log In"**

### Password Recovery

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot your password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. You will receive a link to reset your password
4. The link is valid for 1 hour

## 3. Client Dashboard

Once logged in, you will access your client dashboard where you will find:

### Statistics
- **Completed:** Number of finished jobs
- **Confirmed:** Scheduled and confirmed jobs
- **Pending:** Requests awaiting confirmation
- **Total:** All your jobs

### Calendar
- Monthly view of your scheduled jobs
- Colored dots indicate status:
  - 游릭 Green: Completed
  - 游댯 Blue: Confirmed
  - 游리 Yellow: Pending

### Job List
- Filters by status: All, Completed, Confirmed, Pending
- Detailed information for each job

## 4. Requesting a Service

### Step 1: Select Service
1. Click on **"Request Service"** (green button)
2. Select the type of service you need
3. Click **"Next"**

### Step 2: Date and Time
1. Select the desired date on the calendar
2. Choose your preferred time
3. For **Drone Rental**: select start and end date
4. Review the weather forecast (not available for repairs)
5. Click **"Next"**

### Step 3: Contact Details
1. Complete the form:
   - **Name*** (required)
   - **Email*** (required)
   - **Phone*** (required)
   - **Location*** (required)
   - **Area in hectares** (optional, not applicable for repairs)
   - **Additional notes** (optional)

2. For **Repair and Maintenance**:
   - **Flight hours*** of the drone
   - **Damaged or worn parts*** that need attention

3. Review your request summary
4. Click **"Submit Request"**

### Confirmation
- You will receive an on-screen confirmation
- An email with the details will be sent to you
- The team will contact you within 24 hours

## 5. Available Services

### Drone Fumigation
Precise aerial application of phytosanitary products with state-of-the-art technology.
- Uniform coverage
- Reduced product usage
- Lower environmental impact

### Greenhouse Painting
Application of shading paint on greenhouses using high-precision drones.
- Homogeneous coverage
- Quick application
- No scaffolding or risks

### Aerial Mapping
Detailed terrain analysis with high-resolution images and precise data.
- Orthomosaics
- Vegetation indices
- Problem detection

### Elevation Models
Irrigation and drainage planning with high-precision 3D topographic models.
- DTM (Digital Terrain Model)
- Contour lines
- Slope analysis

### Drone Rental
Professional agricultural drone rental with or without operator.
- By days
- With/without operator
- Training included

### Repair and Maintenance
Specialized technical service for agricultural drones.
- Diagnosis
- Component repair
- Preventive maintenance
- **Required data:** flight hours and affected parts

## 7. Offline Mode

The application works partially without an internet connection:

### When You Are Offline
- **Yellow banner** appears indicating "No connection"
- You can view your saved data (last 24 hours)
- You CANNOT create new requests
- You CANNOT see recent updates

### When You Reconnect
- **Green banner** indicates "Connection restored!"
- Click **"Refresh"** to sync data
- All data is updated automatically
